The Story
"I built this because I know what it feels like to have the drive — and not have the structure."
Yordyn Gonzalez, Founder

I didn't start NextUp Capital from a business book. I started it because I lived the problem. I know what it's like to drive trucks to keep the lights on while figuring out how credit and business really work.

I made mistakes. I learned from them. And once I understood how the system actually worked — how lenders think, what banks look for, how credit really moves — everything changed.

Most entrepreneurs spend months — sometimes years — piecing together an LLC here, a website there, a logo from someone online. By the time they have everything, they've lost momentum, money, and time. And they're still not set up right for funding.

We put it all in one place. Done for you. Properly. So you can focus on building — not filing, calling, or figuring out what comes next.

And because personal credit affects your funding eligibility more than most people realize, every Starter Build includes 6 months of credit repair through NextUp Enterprise — completely free.
